CT correlationRight gluteal region: Large lobulated T2/ IR heterogeneously hyperintense, T1 isointense soft tissue mass lesion measuring approximately 11 x 10.3 x 7.4 cm (CC x TRA x AP) is seen in right gluteal region between right gluteus maximus and medius muscles, just distal to grater sciatic foramen.The lesion is seen in continuity with the right sciatic nerve in proximal gluteal/ thigh regions along the medial aspect. The lesion is abutting posterior capsuloligamentous structures of right hip joint, posterior cortex of posterior wall/ column and muscles. No intra articular extension is seen.The mass lesion is causing significant mass effect and displacement of right gluteus maximus, medius and quadratus femoris muscles. Significant and diffuse edema is seen in gluteus maximus muscle. However, no significant infiltration of the muscles is seen.Small focal extension of the lesion into lesser sciatic foramen is also seen causing significant mass effect over obturator internus muslce. Atrophy and edema of right obturator internus and quadratus femoris muscles are seen. Mild edema is also seen in adjacent fascial planes. The lesion shows moderate heterogeneous enhancement and diffusion restriction (ADC values of 0.9 to 1.08 x 10-3 mm2/s). Patchy relatively less enhancing areas are seen within. No other significant abnormality seen in rest of the visualised soft tissue structures.

## Pattern: Spine Cascade

The classic degenerative spine cascade is the dominant pattern in this dataset:
1. **[[pathologies/disc-desiccation|Disc Desiccation]]** (3507) → earliest sign
2. **[[pathologies/disc-bulge-herniation|Disc Bulge/Herniation]]** (3585) → progression
3. **[[pathologies/annular-tear|Annular Tear]]** (341) → disc integrity failure
4. **[[pathologies/spinal-stenosis|Spinal Stenosis]]** (755) → canal compromise
5. **[[pathologies/cord-signal-changes|Cord Signal Changes]]** (1350) → neural impact
6. **[[pathologies/myelopathy|Myelopathy]]** (279) → clinical myelopathy
